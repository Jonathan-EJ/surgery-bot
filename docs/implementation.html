System Integration

We learned that the difficult part of building a cohesive system is not making the individual parts, but their integration. Implementing the complete functioning robot was a constant challenge on both the hardware and software side and required numerous iterations to have everything working together. Nonetheless, we are pleased with every component of our implementation and how we improved our design along the way :)
